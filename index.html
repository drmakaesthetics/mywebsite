At Dr MAK Aesthetics, all treatments are performed by a GMC-registered doctor with advanced training in aesthetics, facial anatomy and complication management. We use only licensed, medical-grade products and carry emergency reversal agents for all filler treatments. Your safety is never compromised.
Yes. Every new patient receives a full consultation before any treatment is performed. This allows us to assess your skin, understand your concerns, review your medical history and create a safe, personalised treatment plan. We follow a consultation-first policy for all injectable treatments — this is our commitment to ethical, responsible practice.
This varies by treatment. Anti-wrinkle injections typically last 3–4 months. Dermal fillers last 6–18 months depending on area and product. Skin boosters provide visible results for 6–12 months. Skin treatments such as peels and microneedling provide cumulative, long-lasting improvements to skin quality that are maintained with good skincare.
Most treatments involve minimal discomfort. We use topical numbing cream where appropriate, and all injectable products contain lidocaine (a local anaesthetic) to minimise sensation during the procedure. The majority of our clients describe treatments as comfortable and quick.
A GMC-registered doctor has completed 5+ years of medical training before any aesthetic specialisation — bringing deep knowledge of anatomy, skin physiology, pharmacology and the ability to recognise and manage complications. Many aesthetic clinics are run by practitioners who have completed short courses. At Dr MAK Aesthetics, you are in the hands of a qualified doctor at every appointment.
Absolutely. We see many clients specifically for skin health concerns such as acne, pigmentation, rosacea, dehydration and texture issues. A medical skin consultation includes a full assessment, diagnosis and a treatment plan that may include medical skincare prescriptions, in-clinic treatments, or both.
